Current Community Projects

The Masks Up: Masks Down youth program is a twelve-week community led project for students ages 11-17. The program offers youth the space to reflect and journal about life since the “shutdown”. )

Bike Conversion Project is a 3-month hands on learning project for youth 16-24. The project will teach students how to use small tools and attachments to attach a motor on to a manual bike. Students will receive $300 for participating. Please contact kids1@msn.com for more information and registration information.

The BackPack Academy

The Backpack Academy is a math and science educational program. The agency formed in 2011 helping students ages 8-17 in Rainier Valley.
