About Us

April Krubally, MA, Education, is one of the founding Board Members of the Backpack Academy. She served as an Early Learning Specialist for many years. She loves working with youth. She is a tutor, friend, educator, and curious explorer.

Saul Krubally, MA, MHP,CDP is one of the founding Board Members of the Backpack Academy. He is a mental Health Counselor and brings to the Backpack Academy extensive knowledge and experience in assisting families and children in Seattle Public Schools with issues around behavioral and emotional challenges. Saul enjoys traveling and reading.

Kamilah Brown, BA, African American Studies,Public Policy. She is one of the founding Board Members of the Backpack Academy. Kamilah has over 17 years of public policy experience working for a local elected official. She is a longtime community advocate for race and social justice work. Kamilah is a connector, passionate about working with youth and guiding them to their best selves. She enjoys traveling, reading, and trying new foods.

The BackPack Academy

The Backpack Academy is a math and science educational program. The agency formed in 2011 helping students ages 8-17 in Rainier Valley.
