Our Mission

The Backpack Academy is a math and science educational program. The agency formed in 2011 helping students ages 8-17 in Rainier Valley.

Our mission is to help students embrace a relationship with math and recognize that math is within their grasp.The assumption that math is challenging dissipates once students learn that math is malleable. At the Backpack Academy we strive to use math with our students as a dialogue and as a means to focus internal awareness that ultimately will lead to lasting connections.

Current School Projects

The Backpack Academy is working with schools in the Tukwila and Renton School Districts. Students in grades 2-5 explore math in their backyard using objects in nature to make math connections. The Power of Me project at Thorndyke Elementary is an academic math support program and exploring math through a Social Emotional lens.
